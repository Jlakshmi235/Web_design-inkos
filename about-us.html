    Your solution to disaster preparation

Inkos

Keeping track of impending emergency warnings and obtaining information pertaining to safe shelters can be difficult due to the many different resources offered by individual states and counties. Also, it can often be more favorable to seek shelter in nearby buildings and houses than official shelters in some cases. Also, neighbors and people close by may be in a better position to help people in crises but may be unaware of who requires their help. Our project aims to demonstrate a solution which will provide instructions on how to survive in various disasters, information on nearby shelters, and allow people to request for aid to their current location or volunteer to help somebody in need.
